🌿 Our story

Grown with love,

We believe every home deserves a little greenery. Sabrina's Plant Shop was born from a passion for plants and a desire to make them accessible to everyone.

How it all started

It started with a single Monstera on a windowsill. Sabrina had always loved plants but found most plant shops intimidating — full of jargon, overpriced, and not very beginner friendly.

So she decided to build something different. A shop where every plant comes with honest care advice, fair prices, and the kind of warmth you'd expect from a friend who happens to have a very green thumb.

Today Sabrina's Plant Shop offers over 40 carefully selected varieties across four categories — indoor, flower, tree, and garden — with more added every season.
